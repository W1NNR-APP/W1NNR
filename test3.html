W1NNR lets you compete against other players in golf, tennis, and more — with real money on the line, settled on-chain.

How it works:
1️⃣ Create or join a match
2️⃣ Both players deposit stakes
3️⃣ Play your match
4️⃣ Report the W1NNR
5️⃣ Players keep 100% of their winnings 💰
8% platform tournament hosting fee · Settled trustlessly on-chain
⚠️ You'll need:
• MetaMask wallet (or compatible)
• PulseChain network added (see below)
• Some W1N tokens for testing
